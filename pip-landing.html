Why SweatPet works

Built to outsmart your gym anxiety, not just track your workouts.

Gym to Earn

Check in at the CRC and SweatPet knows. Real reps convert straight into food, toys, and habitat upgrades — no cap, no cheating, no faking a set from your dorm room.

Defeat Gym Anxiety

SweatPet gives you a reason to walk in that has nothing to do with how you look doing it. Low-pressure quests ease you into the machines, the free weights, even the group classes — one small errand at a time.

Streak Protection

Midterms happen. SweatPet's grace-period system forgives a missed day without wiping your progress, so one brutal week doesn't undo a month of showing up.
